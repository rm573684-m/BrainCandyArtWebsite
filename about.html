About Brain Candy Art

The Project

Brain Candy Art is a surreal collage project blending layered textures, emotional storytelling, and bold yet human color. Each piece reflects an energy shift — from joy and chaos to memory, dream logic, and absurdity.

The Process

The work draws inspiration from vintage print media, everyday objects, and spontaneous fragments of imagery, all assembled to form new, often unexpected, narratives. Our process is iterative and focused on capturing a singular, strong feeling.
